Privacy Policy for Go Taxi

This Privacy Policy explains how Go Taxi ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App") on iOS and Android platforms. By using the Go Taxi App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Registration Information: When you create an account with Go Taxi, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and password for the purpose of registration and account authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our taxi booking services.
  • To process payments and facilitate transactions.
  • To communicate with you, including sending notifications, updates, and promotional offers.
  • To personalize and enhance your user experience.
  • To monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Taxi Drivers: Your name, phone number, and booking details are shared with taxi drivers to fulfill your ride requests.
  • Payment Processors: We share payment information with trusted third-party payment processors to facilitate transactions.
  • Service Providers: We may use third-party service providers to assist with various aspects of our business, such as analytics, customer support, and marketing.
  • Legal Compliance: We may share your information when required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You may have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access and Correction: You can review and update your account information through the App.
  • Communications Preferences: You can manage your communication preferences in the App settings.
  • Deactivation: You can deactivate your Go Taxi account by contacting us.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or through other means.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@gmail.com.
